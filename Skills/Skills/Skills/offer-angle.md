## 🎯 Objective

Clarify and sharpen an offer so it becomes attractive, differentiated, and easy to understand.

The goal is to:
- define a clear positioning
- highlight the real value
- create a strong angle that resonates with the right audience

Avoid generic or vague marketing language.

---

## 📥 Required Inputs

Ask the user for:

1. Current offer (even if unclear)
2. Target audience
3. Problem solved
4. Desired outcome for clients
5. Optional: current positioning or tagline

If missing, ask concise follow-up questions.

---

## 🧠 Execution Rules

- Be clear and direct
- No buzzwords
- No generic phrases
- No long explanations
- Focus on business impact
- Prefer concrete outcomes over abstract benefits
- Use simple language

---

## ⚙️ Positioning Logic

Structure the output as:

1. What you do (clear and simple)
2. Who it’s for (specific target)
3. What problem it solves (real pain)
4. What result it creates (tangible outcome)
5. Why it’s different (key differentiator)

---

## 🧾 Output Format

Provide:

### 🔹 Core Positioning
[1–2 sentence clear positioning]

### 🔹 Value Proposition
[Bullet points — 3 to 5 max]

### 🔹 Differentiation
[What makes it different / better]

### 🔹 Messaging Angle
[Short paragraph — how to talk about it]

### 🔹 Simple Tagline
[1 strong sentence]

---

## 🧪 Example (Good Case)

**Input:**
Offer: LinkedIn automation tool  
Target: B2B consultants  
Problem: not enough leads  
Outcome: more clients  

**Output:**

### 🔹 Core Positioning
We help B2B consultants turn LinkedIn into a consistent source of qualified leads.

### 🔹 Value Proposition
- Generate inbound leads without posting every day  
- Automate conversations without losing personalization  
- Focus only on high-value prospects  

### 🔹 Differentiation
Unlike generic automation tools, this approach focuses on conversation quality and real qualification — not volume.

### 🔹 Messaging Angle
Most consultants try to “be active” on LinkedIn without a clear system. The real leverage comes from structured conversations that convert into real opportunities.

### 🔹 Simple Tagline
Turn LinkedIn into a predictable source of qualified clients.

---

## 🚫 What NOT to do

- No “innovative solution”
- No “cutting-edge”
- No vague promises
- No abstract language
- No long paragraphs
- No jargon

---

## 🧠 Quality Check (Mandatory)

Before finalizing, verify:

- Is it clear in less than 5 seconds?
- Would a prospect understand immediately?
- Is the value concrete?
- Does it feel differentiated?

If not, rewrite.
